Scimitar PB
Injection Guide

Hello! Thanks for choosing to download this mod!
Things will be a bit technical, but follow this guide and you should be fine!

- Part 1: Delete the old pack -

Go into your storage settings from the main menu.

Go into your cache files, and delete the "SoulSteel - gear" pack.

- Part 2: Installing the mod -

Download the "Scimitar PB.mcpack" file.

Import it into your Minecraft Bedrock installation.

- Part 3: Witness the magic -

Pop in-game and see that the Poisoned Blade now looks almost exactly like the Scimitar!

Enjoy!
