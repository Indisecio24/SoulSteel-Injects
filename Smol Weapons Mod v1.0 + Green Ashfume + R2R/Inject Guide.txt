Smol Weapons Mod + Green Ashfume + Regulars to Relics - v1.0
Injection Guide

Hello! Thanks for choosing to download this mod!
Credit for the Green Ashfume Mod goes to DevGaming.
Things will be a bit technical, but follow this guide and you should be fine!

- Part 1: Delete the old pack -

Go into your storage settings from the main menu.

Go into your cache files, and delete the "SoulSteel - gear" pack.

- Part 2: Installing the mod -

Download the "Smol Weapons Mod v1.0 + Green Ashfume.mcpack" file.

Import it into your Minecraft Bedrock installation.

- Part 3: Witness the magic -

Pop in-game and see all your favourite weapons be smol and cute, with Ashfume now being a lovely green colour, and all your favourite regular armors being given extra flair!

Enjoy!
